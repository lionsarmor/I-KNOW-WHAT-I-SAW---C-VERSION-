Gameplay depth

Items & inventory — HERB heals, a battle ITEM menu entry, maybe a flashlight that matters at night. Biggest bang for making battles more than attack/run.

Boss fight — the TALL ONE with its own art, big stats, a scripted encounter behind a locked door. Gives the game an ending.

Save games — serialize G.player; file on PC, NVS on your ESP32 so the handheld remembers progress across power-off. Huge for a battery device.

Battle variety — enemy special moves, a defend/flee-chance system, status effects (stunned, probed…), XP curve tuning.

Quests/flags — a tiny event-flag array so NPCs react to progress ("you saw it too?!"), doors unlock, the story advances.
